Services

Services we provide.

Here at Kartechner Brothers, we take the utmost pride in our work and value each and everyone of our customers. We strive to always provide the best customer experience throughout all of our services.

Our services include 4" - 15" single wall tile and 12" - 18" dual wall tile installed by a wolfe plow.

Larger tile and pipe installed by excavator.

We can locate and repair old tile systems.

We install tile with great detail to insure that tile is laid on grade. We use GPS to make accurate tile maps. Our 4" and 6" tile is unrolled by a machine mounted stringer to insure that the tile being laid will not be stretched.
